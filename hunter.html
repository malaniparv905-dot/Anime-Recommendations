Story Summary

Hunter x Hunter follows Gon Freecss, a young boy who discovers his father, Ging, is not dead but is a legendary "Hunter". Determined to find his father and become a Hunter himself, Gon leaves his home to take the challenging Hunter Examination. Along the way, he befriends three other aspiring Hunters: the revenge-driven Kurapika, the assassin-in-training Killua, and the aspiring doctor Leorio. The story follows their adventures as they grow, face powerful enemies, and uncover the mysteries of the world and the Hunter Association, which includes the complex and strategic use of a power system called Nen.
