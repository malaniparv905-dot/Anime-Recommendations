Story Summary

Naruto Shippuden follows the teenage ninja Naruto Uzumaki as he returns to his village after two and a half years of training and attempts to achieve his dream of becoming the Hokage, the village leader. The series features Naruto and his allies confronting threats like the mysterious Akatsuki organization, grappling with past traumas, and preparing for an impending Shinobi World War. Naruto's journey includes mastering new abilities, confronting the darkness in his friend Sasuke Uchiha, and ultimately leading the Shinobi Alliance against powerful enemies like Madara Uchiha and Kaguya Otsutsuki.
