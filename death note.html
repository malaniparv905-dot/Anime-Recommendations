Story Summary

Death Note is a supernatural thriller about a high school student, Light Yagami, who finds a notebook that can kill anyone whose name is written in it. He uses it to create a "utopia" by eliminating criminals, but his actions attract the attention of a mysterious genius detective known as L, leading to a battle of wits between the two. The series explores themes of justice, morality, and the corruption of power as Light and L try to outsmart each other.
