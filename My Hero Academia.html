Story Summary

My Hero Academia follows Izuku Midoriya, a boy born without "Quirks" (superpowers) in a world where they are common. Inspired by the number one hero, All Might, Izuku dreams of becoming a hero himself. After a chance encounter, All Might sees Izuku's potential and passes his own Quirk, One for All, to him, allowing Izuku to enroll in the prestigious U.A. High School for hero training. The story follows Izuku's journey of training, growth, and struggle as he learns what it truly means to be a hero, facing both villains and his own limitations along the way.
