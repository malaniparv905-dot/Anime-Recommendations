Story Summary

Black Clover is about Asta, a boy born without magic in a world where magic is everything, who dreams of becoming the Wizard King. He and his rival, the genius mage Yuno, compete to achieve this goal by joining the Magic Knights, with Asta using a rare anti-magic grimoire to overcome his lack of natural power. The story follows their adventures as they battle enemies, form friendships, and challenge the social hierarchy of their world.
