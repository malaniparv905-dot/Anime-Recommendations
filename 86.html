Story Summary

The anime 86 follows the story of the Republic of San Magnolia's war against the Giadian Empire. While the Republic publicizes a "bloodless" war fought by unmanned drones called Juggernauts, they are secretly piloted by the "Eighty-Six," a persecuted group of young men and women from the 86th Sector. The plot focuses on the unlikely pairing of a Republic officer, Lena, who sympathizes with the Eighty-Six, and Shinei "Shin" Nouzen, the commander of the elite Spearhead Squadron. Together, they navigate the brutal battlefield, developing a connection while uncovering the dark truths behind their nation and its fabricated war.
