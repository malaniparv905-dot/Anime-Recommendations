Story Summary

Spy x Family is about a master spy named Twilight, who creates a fake family for a crucial mission called "Operation Strix". He adopts a daughter, Anya, and marries a woman, Yor, to get close to his target, a politician who threatens peace between Ostania and Westalis. Unknown to him, Anya is a telepath and Yor is an assassin with her own hidden life. The three must act like a real family while keeping their true identities secret from one another and the world
