Story Summary

Jujutsu Kaisen follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes the host for a powerful curse named Ryomen Sukuna after eating one of his fingers. To find and consume all of Sukuna's remaining fingers, Yuji joins a secret organization of Jujutsu Sorcerers to eradicate other curses—monstrous manifestations of negative emotions—and ultimately be executed to get rid of Sukuna permanently.
