Story Summary

The plot of Kaiju No. 8 follows Kafka Hibino, a man working a menial job who, after a childhood vow with his friend Mina Ashiro to join the Defense Force, gains the ability to transform into a giant monster called Kaiju No. 8. Because of his newfound power, Kafka tries to secretly join the Defense Force to fulfill his dream and fight alongside Mina, all while navigating the dangers of his new abilities and the organization's pursuit of him.
