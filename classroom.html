Story Summary

Assassination Classroom is a Japanese manga and anime series about a class of junior high students tasked with assassinating their powerful, octopus-like teacher, "Koro-sensei," before he destroys the Earth. The students are considered "misfits" and are taught in a separate classroom, Class 3-E. While their goal is to assassinate him, they also receive a unique education in assassination techniques and regular subjects from their bizarre, Mach-speed-traveling teacher.
