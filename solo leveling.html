Story Summary

Solo Leveling is a fantasy story about Sung Jinwoo, the world's weakest hunter, who gains a unique ability to level up his strength like in a video game after a near-death experience in a mysterious double dungeon. As he completes quests and defeats monsters, he grows from the weakest hunter to one of the strongest, while also uncovering the secrets behind the dungeons and his own power. The series follows his journey as he battles increasingly powerful foes and discovers new abilities, such as necromancy to create an army of shadow soldiers.
